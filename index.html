FAQs

What is SkyBox?

SkyBox is an email platform designed to simplify your communication experience. It offers a range of features to help you manage your emails efficiently.

What are the basic features of SkyBox?

SkyBox provides essential email functions such as composing, sending, receiving, and organizing emails into folders, with added functionalities such as templates, Mail status and this chat.

What are the new features added to SkyBox?

Mail Status: You can track the status of your sent emails, including delivery and read receipts.
Mail Templates: Predefined templates are available for common email types, allowing you to quickly compose messages for various purposes.

How does the Mail Status feature work?

The Mail Status feature allows you to see whether your sent emails have been delivered and if they have been opened by the recipient.

Can I use predefined templates for composing emails in SkyBox?

Yes, SkyBox offers a selection of predefined templates for common email scenarios. You can choose a template and customize it to suit your message.

Is SkyBox free to use?

Yes, SkyBox is a free email platform with basic features available to all users.
